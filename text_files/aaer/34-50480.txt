ORDER VACATING ORDER AND DISMISSING PROCEEDINGS
On August 30, 2004, on the Commission's motion, the United States Court of Appeals for the District of Columbia Circuit remanded Wallace v. Securities and Exchange Commission, No. 03-1350, to the Commission for the purpose of vacating the Commission's order under review therein and dismissing the administrative proceeding.
Accordingly, it is hereby
ORDERED that the Commission's Order of August 20, 2003 is vacated; and it is further
ORDERED that this administrative proceeding is dismissed.
By the Commission.
Jonathan G. Katz
Secretary
